VISION STATEMENT

The vision behind the success of KANAG.(KAan-NAak-Gala)

DR. ANKUR GUPTA

Senior Surgeon ENT

Founder Director

Dr. Ankur Gupta completed his MBBS from LLRM Medical College Meerut with gold medals in medicine and surgery. He completed his Masters from PGIMS Rohtak before moving on to Mumbai. He worked at Tata Memorial Institute in the early 90s.

He gained valuable surgical and management experience under Dr. M.V. Kirtane at KEM and Hinduja Hospitals. He moved on to Meerut his home town started his private practice.

He is the Pioneer in ENT in Meerut who started with Endoscopic, Microscopic diagnostics, and Surgical work. He excelled in audiology with a designated audiology Lab Hearing Solutions. He realized his dream in the form of KANAG.

He is an equally dedicated Philanthropist who has held more than 200 camps and charitable surgeries in memory of his late father Dr. Mahendra Kumar Gupta who was himself EX KGMC pass out.
